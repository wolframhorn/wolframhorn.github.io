Carl-Wolfram Horn

PhD Candidate in Economics

Hi, I am Carl-Wolfram. Welcome to my homepage!

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at the European University Institute (EUI), Florence, Italy. My supervisors are Ramon Marimon and Russell Cooper. During my PhD I have worked as a trainee and research analyst in the Research Division of the European Central Bank.

My research interests include in monetary and fiscal policy, macro finance, and firm heterogeneity.
