Carl-Wolfram Horn

Hi, I am Carl-Wolfram. Welcome to my homepage!

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Centre for Central Banking at the Frankfurt School of Finance and Management. I obtained my Ph.D. in Economics at the European University Institute (EUI), Florence, Italy, in 2024.

I have previously worked as a trainee in the Monetary Policy Outlook Division of the Bank of England and as a trainee and research analyst in the Research Division of the European Central Bank.

My research interests include in monetary and fiscal policy, macro finance, and firm heterogeneity.
